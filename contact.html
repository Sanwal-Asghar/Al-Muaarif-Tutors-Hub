Still Have Questions?

Are there any additional fees?

No, our pricing is completely transparent. You only pay the hourly rate for the sessions you book, with no hidden fees or commitments.

Can I change my plan later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time based on your needs. There are no penalties for changing plans.

Do you offer any discounts?

We offer package discounts when you purchase multiple sessions in advance. We also have special rates for siblings and group sessions.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers. Payment is processed securely through our platform.

What is your cancellation policy?

We understand that schedules can change. You can cancel or reschedule a session up to 24 hours in advance at no charge.
